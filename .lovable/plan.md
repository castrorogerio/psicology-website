
# Psychology Clinic Website — Your Practice

A professional, modern single-page website for your psychology/therapy practice, inspired by the design of sofiacarvalho.pt, with a sage green color palette and clean, trustworthy aesthetic.

## Pages & Sections

### 1. Homepage (Single-Page with Sections)

**Hero Section**
- Full-screen hero with a gradient green background and space for your professional photo
- Headline text and subtext about your services
- Two CTA buttons: "Marcar Consulta Online" (links to WhatsApp) and "Ver Serviços" (scrolls to services)
- Social media links (Instagram, etc.)

**Sobre Mim (About Me)**
- Tabbed section with "Sobre Mim" and "Formação" tabs
- Your personal story and professional background
- List of qualifications, degrees, and certifications

**Serviços (Services)**
- Section header describing your approach
- Featured services with descriptions (e.g., Burnout, PHDA)
- Grid of additional specialties (Ansiedade, Depressão, etc.) as clickable cards
- CTA to contact via WhatsApp for unlisted needs

**Statistics Bar**
- Animated counters: Clients helped, hours of care, years of experience

**Equipa (Team)**
- Team member cards with photo placeholder, name, credentials (OPP number), and short bio
- Scrollable/carousel layout for multiple team members

**Testemunhos (Testimonials)**
- Auto-scrolling carousel of client testimonials
- Each with client name and review text

**Blog Preview**
- Grid of 3-4 latest blog post cards with title, author, excerpt, and "Ler artigo completo" link

**Contacto (Contact)**
- Contact form with name, email, phone, and message fields
- Consent checkbox for data privacy
- Contact info: address, email, phone
- Success/error toast notifications

**Footer**
- CTA banner: "Precisas de ajuda?" with WhatsApp link
- Floating WhatsApp chat button (bottom-right corner)

### 2. Blog Page
- List of all blog posts with title, excerpt, date, and author
- Individual blog post pages with full content

### 3. Service Detail Pages
- Individual pages for each specialty with detailed descriptions

## Design
- **Color palette**: Sage/olive green tones as primary, white and light backgrounds, clean typography
- **Style**: Professional, warm, trustworthy healthcare aesthetic
- **Fully responsive**: Mobile-first with hamburger menu navigation
- **Smooth scroll**: Navigation links scroll to page sections
- **Animations**: Fade-in on scroll, counter animations for stats

## Backend (Lovable Cloud)
- **Blog CMS**: Database table for blog posts so you can manage content
- **Contact form**: Edge function to receive and store contact form submissions
- **Team management**: Database table for team members so you can add/remove psychologists

## Navigation
- Sticky top navigation bar with your practice name, section links (Imprensa, Úteis, Serviços, Blog, Formação, Contactos), and a WhatsApp phone number button
- Mobile hamburger menu
